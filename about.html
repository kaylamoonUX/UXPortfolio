More About Me

Kayla Moon grew up moving from small town to small town in Virginia. This constant change taught her to appreciate and embrace the diversity of people. As she grew, two more core principles became evident: her love for art and her desire to help others.

These principles guided Kayla through her college studies in graphic design. However, she quickly realized that traditional graphic design, particularly logo design, didn't sit right with her. During her second year, she discovered UX design and found her true passion.

Kayla continued honing her graphic design skills by working for her university, but her heart was set on UX. When the UX designer position opened up, she pursued it persistently until she secured the role.

Now, with over a year of experience on a UX team and more than three years in freelance design overall, Kayla combines her talents in creative problem-solving and user-centered design. Not one to shy away from challenges, she also launched an online store selling handcrafted journals and stationery, created using her grandfather's machinary.

Kayla throws herself fully in every project she takes on. Whether she's building this website from scratch, designing new journals for her business, or refining a website's navigation bar, Kayla is all in all the time.
